Current build date: October 8th 2017

Individual C# Scripts can be found at: FPS_Experimentation\Hitscan_FPS\Assets\Scripts


Playable .exe of the current build is at: FPS_Experimentation\Hitscan_FPS\CURRENT_BUILD
-Using Windowed mode of the .exe is recommended

CONTROLS:
-Standard FPS movement: WASD movement + mouse to look around
-Holding Space Bar consumes jetpack fuel to rise vertically
-Pressing Left Shift gives a short burst of upward momentum
-Pressing E shoots a projectile that knocks back all nearby non-environmental objects
-Left click shoots hitscan-style
